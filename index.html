it all started in 2021

I met Abed Goal during high school, and at first, we battled each other in the game. But one day, we discovered that playing side by side was a thousand times more fun. From that moment, we dominated Europe and lifted every title we dreamed of. Five unforgettable years — full of highs and lows, challenges and victories. We played everywhere: on PlayStations, in houses, on streets, at the beach, and even inside our little shoe shop. We played under the rain, with heavy clouds filling the sky, never caring about anything except the game. Our real mission was hidden in our names: I was there to assist, and he was there to score the goal. Together, we pushed each other higher — to win more, to dream bigger, and to enjoy every second of the journey.
